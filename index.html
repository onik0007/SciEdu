Courses We Offer

PEP

PEP (Primary Exit Profile)assesses students' readiness to move from primary to secondary education and covers areas such as literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

CSEC

CSEC assesses High school students in the Caribbean, evaluating their knowledge and skills across various subjects at the secondary level.

CAPE

CAPE assesses secondary education students in the Caribbean, evaluating their proficiency in diverse subjects after two years of advanced level studies.
